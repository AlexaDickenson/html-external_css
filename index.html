What I Love About Pilates:


  • It creates a strong mind-body connection. Some people lack mind-body connection, and have trouble with coordination and completing daily activities because of that.


  • Pilates can increase your spinal mobility. Many don't understand what spinal mobility is or how important it is, but having good spinal mobility can prevent lots of issues (especially ones related to back pain) as we age.


  • Everything you do in Pilates helps to engage your core. Core strength is especially important if you have back pain or struggle with posture. Pilates was originally invented as a form of physical therapy to help with these issues.


  • Many exercises in Pilates focus on joint mobility. For example, certain exercises focus on moving the hip socket in a way that we aren't normally used to. By strengthening this area, you can prevent issues that may require surgery or physical therapy in the future.
